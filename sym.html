SYM

An automated Micro savings app

SYM(Save Your Money), a savings manager app is the flagship product of Codeister Technologies. Savings management is an area which is of utmost importance to us and something which we all wish to master. Yet few of us actually do. This is mainly because of the extensive planning saving requires. Our app SYM will help each and every person save and manage their expenses. SYM will track your expenses and spending based on your credit/debit card receipts. It also provides an option to manually enter your expenses for transactions done without using a card. Based on this information the app will suggest the amount to be saved. If this is acceptable to the user, the money will be moved to a secure account. Another feature of SYM is that you can set goals for saving. This feature allows you to set a time period for saving towards a particular goal (amount) and the app will transfer certain amounts in order to reach your goal by the specified time period. So this app will help you to plan and save easily without putting in any effort.
